Real impact made possible through collaboration

Fresh Start Community Kitchen

Chicago, IL
Since 2023

Fresh Start Community Kitchen serves over 200 meals daily to homeless and low-income individuals. Before joining CarePlate, they often struggled with consistent food sources and quality.

Since partnering with CarePlate, they've been able to receive high-quality food from 15 local restaurants, increasing their meal capacity by 40% while significantly improving meal variety and nutritional value.

"CarePlate has transformed our operation. We now serve more nutritious meals with greater variety, and our community members often comment on how much better the food has become. The platform's reliability means we can plan better and focus on other aspects of service."

Impact: 15,000+ meals served, $25,000 in food costs saved, and a 30% increase in regular attendees.

Green Table Restaurant Group

Boston, MA
Since 2022

Green Table Restaurant Group operates five popular farm-to-table restaurants across Boston. Despite their best efforts to minimize waste, they found themselves discarding significant amounts of high-quality food at the end of each day.

After joining CarePlate, they've been able to redirect nearly 90% of their surplus food to local shelters and community centers, dramatically reducing their environmental impact while making a positive difference in their community.

"As a business focused on sustainability, finding CarePlate was a game-changer. Our staff morale has improved knowing our food isn't wasted, and we've actually seen an increase in customer loyalty as we share our impact metrics. It's a win-win situation that aligns perfectly with our values."

Impact: 8 tons of food rescued, 65% reduction in food waste costs, and a feature in Local Business Magazine's "Sustainability Leaders" issue.
